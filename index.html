About Us

Our Vision

To build a responsive, connected donor community where available blood units match urgent medical needs instantly, eliminating shortages and delays for patients in critical condition.

Our Mission

To streamline donor registration, scheduling, and notifications; empower hospitals with real-time inventory visibility; and inspire individuals to become repeat donors through transparent impact tracking and compassionate storytelling.
